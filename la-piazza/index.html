Our Story

A family tradition spanning generations

Since 1987, La Piazza has been the home of Italy's most authentic flavors in the heart of the city. Founded by the Rossi family, our restaurant was born from a dream of sharing recipes passed down from generation to generation.

Every dish we serve tells a story: fresh ingredients selected daily, handmade pastas, and sauces prepared with the same dedication as over three decades ago.
